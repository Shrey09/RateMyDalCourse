About Us

We provide information on every subject of Computer Science at the Dalhousie University by providing course ratings and discussion forum. The idea is to convey the information of different courses in such a manner so that student can easily decide whether the subject will be helpful to them or not. RateMyDalCourse also eradicates the communication barrier among the student and resolve their queries using the discussion forum.
